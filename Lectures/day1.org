* Transient Climate status

\begin{align*}
R \frac{dT}{dt} = S_0 (1-\alpha(T)) - \sigma T^{4} \\
\alpha(T) = \frac{\alpha _{w} + \alpha _{c}}{2} + \frac{\alpha _{w} - \alpha _{c}}{2} \text{tanh}(\frac{T}{\Delta T}) \\
t = R \int_{ T _{0} }^{ T} \frac{1}{S_0 ( 1- \alpha(T')) - \sigma T '^{4} } dT' 
.
\end{align*}
